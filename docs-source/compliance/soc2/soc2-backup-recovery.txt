Compliance Family: SOC 2

Source: AICPA Trust Services Criteria - A1.3 (https://www.aicpa-cima.com/resources/landing/system-and-organization-controls-soc-suite-of-services)
Date Collected: 27/03/2026
Type: Official Documentation


A1.2 The entity authorizes, designs, develops or acquires, implements, operates, approves, maintains, and monitors environmental protections, software, data back-up processes, and recovery infrastructure to meet its objectives.
Points of Focus:
- Backs Up Data: The entity maintains data back-up processes to protect data from loss and to enable recovery.
- Tests Recovery Plan: The entity tests the recovery plan and processes at regular intervals to determine whether the systems and data can be recovered to meet the entity's objectives.
- Stores Backups Securely: The entity stores data backups off-site or in a geographically distinct location to protect against regional disasters.
