CC6.5 The entity discontinues logical and physical protections over physical assets only after the ability to read or recover data and software from those assets has been diminished and is no longer required to meet the entity's objectives.
Points of Focus:
- Retains Data: The entity retains data only as long as necessary to fulfill its purposes and legal or regulatory requirements.
- Disposes of Data Securely: The entity securely disposes of data and software when they are no longer needed.

C1.2 The entity disposes of confidential information to meet the entity's objectives related to confidentiality.
Points of Focus:
- Destroys Confidential Information: The entity destroys confidential information when it is no longer needed to meet the entity's objectives.
