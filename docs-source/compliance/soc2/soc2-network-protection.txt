CC6.6 The entity implements logical access security measures to protect against threats from sources outside its boundaries.
Points of Focus:
- Restricts Access to the Network: The entity restricts access to its network boundaries to authorized personnel and devices.
- Uses Firewalls and Intrusion Detection Systems: The entity implements boundary protection systems, such as firewalls and intrusion detection systems, to monitor and control communications at external boundaries.

CC6.8 The entity implements controls to prevent or detect the introduction of unauthorized or malicious software to meet the entity's objectives.
Points of Focus:
- Restricts Unauthorized Connections: The entity restricts the connection of unauthorized devices and software to the network.
