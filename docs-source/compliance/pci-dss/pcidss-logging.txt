Requirement 10: Log and Monitor All Access to System Components and Cardholder Data.

10.2 Audit logs are implemented to support the detection of anomalies and suspicious activities, and the forensic analysis of events.
10.2.1 Audit logs capture all individual user accesses to cardholder data.
10.2.2 Audit logs record all actions taken by any individual with administrative access, including any interactive use of application or system accounts.
10.2.3 Audit logs record all access to audit logs.
10.2.4 Audit logs record all invalid logical access attempts.

10.3 Audit logs are protected from destruction and unauthorized modifications.
10.3.1 Read access to audit logs is limited to those with a job-related need.
10.3.2 Audit log files are protected to prevent modifications to existing data.
10.3.3 Audit log files are promptly backed up to a secure, central, internal log server or other media that is difficult to modify.

10.5 Audit log history is retained and available for analysis.
10.5.1 Retain audit log history for at least 12 months, with at least the most recent three months immediately available for analysis.
