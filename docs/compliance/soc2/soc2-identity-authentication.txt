CC6.2 Prior to issuing system credentials and granting system access, the entity evaluates the identity of internal and external users.
Points of Focus:
- Identifies Users: The entity identifies users who require access to the system based on their role and business requirements.
- Evaluates Identity: The entity evaluates the identity of users to verify they are who they claim to be prior to issuing system credentials.

CC6.3 The entity authorizes, modifies, or removes access to data, software, functions, and other IT resources to enable individuals to meet the entity's objectives.
Points of Focus:
- Implements Authentication: The entity implements robust authentication mechanisms (such as complex passwords, tokens, or biometrics) to authenticate users.
- Manages Credentials: The entity securely manages credentials throughout their lifecycle, including issuance, storage, rotation, and revocation.
- Uses Multi-Factor Authentication: The entity implements multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all remote access to the network and for all administrative or privileged access to system components.
