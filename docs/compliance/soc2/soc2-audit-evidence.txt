CC4.1 The entity selects, develops, and performs ongoing and/or separate evaluations to ascertain whether the components of internal control are present and functioning.
Points of Focus:
- Performs Ongoing Evaluations: The entity performs ongoing evaluations to ascertain whether the components of internal control are present and functioning.
- Evaluates Objectively: The entity evaluates internal control objectively to provide assurance that the components are functioning.

CC7.3 The entity evaluates security events to determine whether they could or have resulted in a failure of the entity to meet its objectives (security incidents) and, if so, takes actions to prevent or address such failures.
Points of Focus:
- Analyzes Security Events: The entity analyzes security events to determine whether they represent security incidents.
- Documents Security Incidents: The entity documents security incidents and the actions taken to address them.
