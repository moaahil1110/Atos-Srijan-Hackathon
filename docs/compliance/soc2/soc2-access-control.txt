CC6.1 The entity implements logical access security software, infrastructure, and architectures over protected information assets to protect them from security events to meet the entity's objectives.

Points of Focus:
- Registers and Authorizes Users: The entity registers new internal and external users and authorizes their access based on defined roles.
- Grants Access: The entity grants logical access to IT infrastructure and software to authorized users based on the principle of least privilege.
- Prevents Unauthorized Access: The entity restricts logical access to prevent unauthorized access to protected assets.
- Removes Access: The entity removes access for terminated or transferred users promptly to prevent unauthorized ongoing access.
- Reviews Access: The entity reviews user access privileges at regular intervals to determine whether access remains appropriate and authorized.
