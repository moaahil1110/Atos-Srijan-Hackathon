Requirement 11: Test Security of Systems and Networks Regularly.

11.2 Wireless access points are identified and monitored.
11.2.1 Interactions with wireless access points are monitored to detect and identify all authorized and unauthorized wireless access points on a quarterly basis.

11.3 External and internal vulnerabilities are regularly identified, prioritized, and addressed.
11.3.1 Internal vulnerability scans are performed at least once every three months.
11.3.2 External vulnerability scans are performed at least once every three months by a PCI SSC Approved Scanning Vendor (ASV).

Requirement 12: Support Information Security with Organizational Policies and Programs.

12.5 PCI DSS scope is documented and validated.
12.5.2 PCI DSS scope is documented and validated by the entity at least once every 12 months and upon significant change to the in-scope environment.
